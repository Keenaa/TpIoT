Tilemap generator

Load a map from the Highmaps Map Collection and convert it to a tilemap. The algorithm will output a best guess for the loaded map, which can then be edited to produce the desired result. Play around with the algorithm parameters to find the best combination for your map. Inverting the chart will swap the X and Y axes, mostly useful for changing the orientation of honeycomb charts.
